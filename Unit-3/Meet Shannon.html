Meet Shannon

Hi, my name is Shannon. I live in California, the Bay Area to be exact.

If I had to choose a person to meet, I'd choose Ursula Burns. She was the first Black woman to become the CEO of a Fortune 500 company — Xerox! Isn’t that inspiring?

I love all things tech. I plan to take every computer science course offered at school!

Tech is amazing because it lets us create. My dream is to build something that changes the world.
